Two-hour Port Cruise with Live Commentary

Admire the sights and skyline of Hamburg on this two-hour cruise

  • Instant confirmation
  • Free cancellation

This two-hour cruise around the Port of Hamburg allows you to discover the vibrant docks, maritime flair and the interesting crowds at the second-largest port in Europe. From the state-of-the-art container complexes to the small fishing towns along the Elbe and the famous fish market – you will be able to observe the contrast between the past and present.

The captain will provide commentary throughout the cruise, with fascinating insights and facts about the history of Hamburg. You will be able to admire the sights and skyline of Hamburg from the water.

Meeting point

St. Pauli Boarding Bridge 1, Hamburg

Please be at the ticket pavilion of boarding bridge-1 at least 15 minutes before the tour starts.

The cruise will depart from boarding bridges 4–10.

Why Visit

  • Stunning views of the skyline and maritime flair
  • A guided two-hour cruise around the port of Hamburg
  • Interesting facts and insights about the city
